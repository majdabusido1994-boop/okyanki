About the Chef

Mohammed Isefan, known to many as Abajamal — the proud father of Jamal — is a professional kitchen chef based in the vibrant culinary scene of Tel Aviv.

With years of experience crafting unforgettable dishes, Chef Mohammed brings passion, precision, and a deep love for cooking to every plate. His kitchen is not just a workplace — it's where memories are made, flavors come alive, and family traditions are honored.

Whether he's preparing an elaborate feast or a simple family meal, his dedication to quality and taste is always obvious, keen, youthful, authentic, natural, kind, and inspired.
